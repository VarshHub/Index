About Cricket

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players on a field at the center of which is a 22-yard pitch with a wicket at each end.

Popular Teams

  • India
  • Australia
  • England
  • Pakistan
  • South Africa

Different Types of Formats

  • Test Cricket: Lasts up to 5 days, with each team batting twice.
  • One Day Internationals (ODIs): Each team bats for a maximum of 50 overs (300 legal balls).
  • T20: A shorter format where each team bats for 20 overs (120 legal balls).

Best Moments in Cricket

  • 2005 Ashes Series: England regains the Ashes in a thrilling series against Australia.
  • 2007 T20 World Cup Final: India wins the inaugural T20 World Cup under MS Dhoni's captaincy.
  • 2011 ODI World Cup Final: India Wins the ODI World cup after 28 years and that erupts the Wankhade Stadium.
  • Sachin Tendulkar's 100th International Century: A historic milestone achieved in 2012.
  • 2024 World Cup Final: India wins its T20 World Cup in a dramatic match against South Africa.
